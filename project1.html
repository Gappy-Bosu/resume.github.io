3D Models & Textures






















In 2022 and 2023, I created some mods for a game called "Garry's Mod".


These mods contain 3D Models of Clone Troopers from the Star Wars TV show, I used substance painter to add texture, shading, dirt, and wear & tear to these models to make them look more realistic while still maintaining their cartoon-esque appearance.


  • CGI HD: 88th Infantry Corps [TC13u]
  • STAR WARS The Clone Wars: Phase 2 7th Legion
  • STAR WARS: 501st Legion Mixer [CUSTOM]
